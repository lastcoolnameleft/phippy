Where did this idea come from?

This code and idea behind this was inspired by Duckiehunt.

My lovely wife and I were away on our honeymoon and our friends conspired a huge rubber duckie invasion. We came back to 1000+ duckies in the house. There were even ~300 on the bed. Days later I went into work to find ~200 more there. It was pretty absurd and hilarious. We would expect nothing else from our friends.

Why are you doing this?
With the donation of Phippy and Friends licensing to the CNCF, and with my existing framework for tracking rubber ducks, this seemed like a natural expansion.
